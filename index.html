By Juana Moreno
SCS ICT CEW CYB

Tuesday May 26 is our kickoff day! We are still working on the details, so please check this page in the next few days, as more specific instructions will be posted.

Unless otherwise noted, all the meetings will be carried out through Zoom. If you do not have a Zoom account, you may need to create one.

Click here for specific instructions for EBRPSS teachers

Go to zoom and create an account. If you enter your ebrschools.org address, the system will ask you if this is for "official school purposes" or for "personal purposes". Select "personal purposes" and enter a new password. It is recommended that you use a password that is different from your normal ebrschools password, to protect you from possible liability. After this, you can use Zoom normally with your ebrschools email.
